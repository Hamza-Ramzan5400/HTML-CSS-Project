Imran Khan

Iron Man Of Pakistan

Imran khan is a former crickter of Pakistan and a politician who served as the 22nd prime minister of pakistan from 2018 to 0222. He is the founder and chairman of pakistan tehreek-e-insaf (PTI) and the formr captain of the pakistan national cricket team from 1971 to 1992. He is also a philanthropist and the founder of the shaukat khanum memorial cancer hospital & research centre, which is the first of its kind in pakistan and the largest cancer hospital in the country. He is also the founder of Namal University, which is a private university in pakistan that offers higher education in various fields of study. Imran khan is known for his charismatic personality and his ability to connect with the people of pakistan.He is also known for his strong stance against corruption and his commitment to improving the lives of people in pakistan. He has been a vocal advocate for education and healthcare reform in the country.
